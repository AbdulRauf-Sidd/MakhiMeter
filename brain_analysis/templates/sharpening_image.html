Sharpening Image



The sharpening module is a key technique in image processing, designed to enhance the clarity and detail of an image by emphasizing edges and fine features. By increasing the contrast along boundaries, it helps make important elements more distinguishable, improving overall image quality. This is especially crucial in fields like scientific imaging, where clear, detailed visuals are essential for accurate analysis. Sharpening algorithms, such as the Unsharp Mask or High-Pass Filtering, help highlight subtle features, making it easier to detect fine structures and extract meaningful insights from images.
